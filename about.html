About me

A bit more context about who I am, what I’m studying, and how design fits in.

Background

I’m an Information Science student with a focus on human-centered design. I’m interested in how interfaces can support real people with real needs, especially in wellness and education spaces.

In addition to my coursework, I’ve been exploring interaction design, user research, and prototyping tools as I move toward a career in UX/product design.

What I’m learning

  • Structuring content clearly with HTML
  • Styling layouts with modern CSS
  • Planning user flows before jumping into visuals
  • Communicating design decisions in plain language

Looking ahead

As my skills grow, I’m excited to create more detailed case studies, experiment with interaction patterns, and build projects that connect digital tools with real-world experiences—especially in yoga, fitness, and student life.

This site represents an earlier stage of my portfolio. My more advanced work includes richer visuals, prototypes, and research, but I’m glad you’re seeing where it started.
